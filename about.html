Our mission

Bridging the gap between manufacturer and retailer

Premium Pioneers Sales Group was founded on the belief that great products deserve great distribution. Too many manufacturers spend years trying to break into retail channels that could be opened in months with the right relationships and know-how.

PPSG provides that bridge — handling everything from product curation and channel development to logistics and marketing support, so manufacturers can focus on what they do best.
